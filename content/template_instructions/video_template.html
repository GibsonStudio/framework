Video Templates

Our current standard for CBT production is a mixture of HTML and video. Many applications are capable of producing video (Flash, Premier) and you can use whatever you feel best suits the project / scene. Rather that have templates for every possible application, I will define the sizes and colours to be used, and where to find templates for some apps.

Templates

Text Boxes

We can use 3 styles of text box, vertical, horizontal and none:

Horizontal: 100% wide x 120px high. Positioned at the bottom of the window.

120px high

Vertical: 356px wide x 100% high. Positioned on the left of the window.

356px wide

None: No text box is used, leaving the whole screen for graphics / large text.
