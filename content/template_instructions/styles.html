Positioning Elements

Elements can be positioned with the classes:

  • content_left: Aligns the contents to the left - text-align: left;
  • content_right: Aligns the contents to the right - text-align: right;
  • content_center: Aligns contents to the center - text-align: center;
  • content_top: Aligns contents to top - vertical-align: top;
  • content_bottom: Aligns contens to bottom - vertical-align: bottom;
  • left: Aligns the element to the left - float: left;
  • right: Aligns the element to the right - float: right;
  • center: Aligns element to center of container - margin: auto;
